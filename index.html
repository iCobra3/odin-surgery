what is surgery?

Surgery is a medical specialty that involves the physical manipulation or removal of tissues, organs, or structures within the body to treat disease, injury, or deformity. It can be used for diagnostic, therapeutic, or reconstructive purposes and can be performed on almost every part of the body.
